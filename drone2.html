DESCRIPTION

The Youngrc F450 Drone Frame Kit is a versatile and durable 4-axis quadcopter frame designed for building custom drones. With a 450mm diagonal motor-to-motor distance, this frame is suitable for a wide range of drone applications, including aerial photography, FPV (First-Person View), and racing drones. It is constructed from high-quality, lightweight materials such as carbon fiber or plastic, providing both strength and low weight for better flight performance. The modular design allows easy assembly, and it supports a variety of motor sizes, flight controllers, and batteries, making it a great choice for hobbyists and professionals alike. The frame also offers compatibility with different propeller sizes, ensuring flexibility for various flight setups.

  • SPECIFICATIONS
    • Frame Size: 450mm (diagonal motor-to-motor distance)
    • Material: Typically carbon fiber or durable plastic for a lightweight yet strong structure
    • Motor Mounting: Standard motor mounts for 22xx or 23xx sized motors
    • Frame Type: 4-axis quadcopter (supports 4 motors and propellers)
    • Flight Controller Compatibility: Compatible with popular flight controllers (e.g., Naze32, Pixhawk, KK2, F3/F4 boards)
    • Propeller Size: Supports 8-12 inch propellers (depending on motor configuration)
    • Battery Compatibility: Suitable for 3S-4S LiPo batteries
    • Weight: Lightweight design for better maneuverability and longer flight times
    • Landing Gear: Includes basic landing gear for stability during landings
    • Camera Mount: Mounting points available for FPV cameras and small action cameras (GoPro, etc.)
    • Assembly: Modular design for easy and flexible assembly and maintenance
    • Applications: Ideal for hobbyists, aerial photography, FPV flying, and drone racing
    • Durability: Strong and durable frame designed for both beginners and experienced drone enthusiasts
    • Operating Temperature: Typically operates within -10°C to 50°C range, depending on the frame material and components
