DESCRIPTION

The MC33172 is a low-power, dual bipolar operational amplifier designed for precision signal processing in a variety of applications. It features a wide operating voltage range and low input offset voltage, making it ideal for use in low-power circuits that require high accuracy. With its low quiescent current, the MC33172 is well-suited for battery-operated devices, instrumentation, and audio applications. It offers a wide gain bandwidth product and is designed to operate in both single and dual-supply configurations, providing versatility and efficiency. This operational amplifier is commonly used in signal conditioning, filters, and amplification tasks.

  • SPECIFICATIONS
    • Type: Low-power dual bipolar operational amplifier
    • Operating Voltage: 3V to 32V (single supply) or ±1.5V to ±16V (dual supply)
    • Input Offset Voltage: Low (typically 50mV)
    • Quiescent Current: Low, ideal for battery-operated devices
    • Gain Bandwidth Product: 1.5 MHz (typical)
    • Output Voltage Swing: Close to ground (rail-to-rail output)
    • Package Types: Available in 8-pin PDIP, SOIC, and other standard packages
    • Common-Mode Input Voltage Range: Includes ground (rail-to-rail input)
    • Applications: Signal conditioning, audio amplification, filters, precision measurement
    • Operating Temperature Range: -40°C to +85°C
    • Compatible with single or dual supply configurations
