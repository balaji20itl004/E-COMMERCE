DESCRIPTION

The 400mAh 3.7V single-cell LiPo (Lithium Polymer) battery is a compact and lightweight power source ideal for small electronic devices, drones, wearable technology, and other portable projects. It provides a nominal voltage of 3.7V with a capacity of 400mAh, offering a good balance between runtime and size. This battery is designed for high energy density and efficiency, and its flexible design makes it suitable for tight spaces. The LiPo battery is rechargeable, providing long-lasting power with proper care and charging, and is compatible with a variety of charging circuits.

    • Rechargeable: Yes, with proper charging and care
    • Design: Lightweight, compact, and flexible for tight spaces
