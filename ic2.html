DESCRIPTION

The 4HC161 is a 4-bit presettable synchronous binary counter IC, designed to provide reliable and fast counting functionality in digital circuits. It features a synchronous operation, meaning that all its flip-flops are triggered simultaneously by the clock signal, allowing for precise counting without any timing issues between stages. The IC is capable of both up and down counting, and its outputs are available in binary format. Additionally, the 4HC161 allows for direct presetting of the counter to any 4-bit value, providing flexibility for specific counting sequences. This counter IC is commonly used in applications such as frequency division, timers, and digital clocks.

  • SPECIFICATIONS
    • Type: 4-bit presettable synchronous binary counter
    • Number of Bits: 4-bit (provides a range of 0 to 15 in binary)
    • Operating Voltage: 2V to 6V
    • Clock Input: Synchronous operation, all flip-flops are triggered simultaneously
    • Counting Direction: Up and Down counting modes
    • Presetting: Direct presetting of counter to any 4-bit value
    • Output Type: Binary output (Q0-Q3)
    • Active Low Reset: Allows resetting the counter to 0
    • Applications: Frequency division, digital clocks, timers, and counters
    • Package Types: Available in standard 16-pin Dual In-line Package (DIP), SOIC, etc.
    • Operating Temperature Range: -40°C to +85°C
