DFRobot Fermion: AS7341 11-Channel Visible Light Sensor (Breakout)

Now ₹ 1749.00

DESCRIPTION

The DFRobot Fermion AS7341 11-Channel Visible Light Sensor is a compact and high-precision sensor designed for spectral analysis and color detection applications. It features 11 independent channels for detecting visible light wavelengths (VIS) and near-infrared (NIR) light, offering detailed spectral data. The sensor supports I2C communication for seamless integration with microcontrollers, making it ideal for applications in color recognition, environmental sensing, and scientific research. With its small form factor and advanced functionality, it is a powerful tool for projects requiring accurate light measurement and spectral analysis.

  • SPECIFICATIONS
    • Spectral Channels: 11 independent channels (visible light and near-infrared)
    • Communication Interface: I2C
    • Operating Voltage: 3.3V to 5V
    • Detection Range: Visible light (VIS) and near-infrared (NIR)
    • Applications: Color recognition, spectral analysis, environmental sensing, and scientific research
    • Compact Size: Small form factor for easy integration
    • High Precision: Accurate light measurement and analysis
    • Manufacturer: DFRobot Fermion series
