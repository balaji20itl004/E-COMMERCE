DESCRIPTION

The Panasonic Pro AA Ni-MH 4-Battery Pack offers a reliable and high-performance solution for powering a variety of devices that use AA batteries, such as cameras, toys, flashlights, and remote controls. These rechargeable Nickel-Metal Hydride (Ni-MH) batteries provide a capacity of 2500mAh, delivering long-lasting power and reducing the need for disposable batteries. Designed for durability and efficiency, the Panasonic Pro AA batteries have a low self-discharge rate, allowing them to hold charge for extended periods.
