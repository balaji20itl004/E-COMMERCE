DESCRIPTION

The Raspberry Pi 4 Model B with 4GB of RAM is a powerful and versatile single-board computer designed for a wide range of applications, from learning to code to developing complex projects. It features a quad-core ARM Cortex-A72 CPU running at 1.5GHz, providing significant performance improvements over its predecessors. The 4GB of LPDDR4-3200 SDRAM offers ample memory for multitasking and running more demanding applications. This model includes dual-band 2.4/5.0 GHz wireless LAN, Bluetooth 5.0, and Gigabit Ethernet, ensuring fast and reliable connectivity options. It also supports dual 4K HDMI display outputs, making it suitable for multimedia applications. The board comes equipped with two USB 3.0 ports and two USB 2.0 ports, allowing for a variety of peripheral connections, and the 40-pin GPIO header enables extensive hardware interfacing for custom projects.


  • SPECIFICATIONS

    • CPU: Quad-core ARM Cortex-A72 (ARM v8) 64-bit SoC @ 1.5GHz
    • RAM: 4GB LPDDR4-3200 SDRAM
    • Dual-band 2.4GHz and 5.0GHz IEEE 802.11b/g/n/ac wireless LAN
    • Bluetooth 5.0, BLE
    • Gigabit Ethernet
    • 2 × USB 3.0 ports, 2 × USB 2.0 port
    • 2 × micro HDMI ports (up to 4Kp60 supported)
    • 5V DC via USB-C connector (minimum 3A)
    • Enabled (requires separate PoE HAT)
