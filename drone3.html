DESCRIPTION

The DJI FPV Drone Foldable Landing Gear is an essential accessory designed to enhance the takeoff and landing experience for the DJI FPV drone. This landing gear is foldable, allowing it to be compact and easy to store when not in use. Made from durable materials, it provides added stability and protection to the drone during landings, reducing the risk of damage to the body or camera. The foldable design also ensures that the landing gear does not interfere with the drone's flight or transport, providing convenience for both storage and portability. These landing gear spare parts are perfect for replacing damaged landing gear or upgrading the original equipment, ensuring smooth, safe landings in various environments.
